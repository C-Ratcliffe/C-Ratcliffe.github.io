Neuroscience by Numbers: Theory through Practice

A combination blog, portfolio, and informative resource

About the author:

Corey Ratcliffe is a Phd Student who is currently working on developing network models of epileptogenesis in neurocysticercosis. Based at the University of Liverpool, Corey is collecting data at NIMHANS Bangalore.

Included on this site is:

  • An overview of and introduction to the work I have done since starting my PhD in neuroimaging, which has focused on imaging neural networks in Epilepsy and Neurocysticercosis
  • Supporting information for presentations and posters
  • A book and music journal
  • A list of recommended software for a wide variety of functions, on Windows, Linux (Ubuntu), and sometimes Android
  • Links to software resources
  • An online C.V.
  • Please excuse the commit history, this is a work in progress

All opinions presented are solely my own; recommendations of media and resources are made independent of any ideologies/beliefs those involved in the production may hold
