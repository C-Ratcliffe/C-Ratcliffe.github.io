Neuroscience by Numbers: Theory through Practice

A combination blog, portfolio, and informative resource

About the author:

Corey Ratcliffe is a PhD Student who is currently working towards developing network models of epileptogenesis in neurocysticercosis. Based at the University of Liverpool, collecting data at NIMHANS Bangalore.
The intention for this site is threefold; for it to function as a professional-academic portfolio, a resource for those new to modern neuroscience practices and methods, and an introduction to me outside of academia, in the form of book & music blog. To this end, please feel free to get in touch with me--via my contact address in the page footer--if you'd like any further information regarding my work or my interests. Cheers!

Site Contents:

  • Books: Some recommendations for reading material; mostly fiction, including satire and fantasy
  • C.V.: A professional summary of myself, also available as a pdf
